Zesty southwest corn salad

Description

Easy and delicious, this salad is refreshing with your summer grilled dishes.

The perfect barbeque dish to add some freshness to your juicy bbq meat

Ingredients

  • 2 (16 ounce) packages frozen corn, thawed and drained
  • 1 ½ cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup diced green bell pepper
  • ⅓ cup diced red onion
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons freshly chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  • Step 1: Combine corn, tomatoes, bell pepper, and onion in a large bowl.
  • Step 2: Whisk olive oil, cilantro, vinegar, lime juice, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and cayenne together in a small bowl. Add dressing to corn mixture just before serving and toss well.
